Choosing the right Linux distro

Everywhere you go on Internet, when you see someone talking about his distro, you’ll feel like they’re talking about their wife or their religion. It is THAT serious. When you open the terminal, you might’ve think of something like: “Oh that looks difficult, perhaps this “X” button on the window will close the damn thing.” We are not going to talk about the command line in this article, no not yet.
